<p class='hn-text-en'>A new analysis shows fossil fuels represent about 40% of freight tonnage but account for roughly half the energy use, underscoring efficiency gains and decarbonization opportunities in shipping. The finding suggests focusing on vessel design, routes, and energy mix to cut emissions.</p>
<p class='hn-text-zh'>研究显示化石燃料约占货运吨位的40%，却约占一半的能源消耗，凸显航运业在减排方面的潜在改进点。应聚焦船舶设计、航线优化和能源结构以降低排放。</p>
