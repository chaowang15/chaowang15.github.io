<p class='hn-text-en'>Binary dependencies are the unseen web of libraries pulled in by software builds; Many projects rely on transitive dependencies that escape notice. Pinpointing these hidden dependencies improves security, licensing, and reproducibility, and requires tooling to analyze binaries and their dynamic/static dependencies.</p>
<p class='hn-text-zh'>二进制依赖是软件构建中看不见的库网络，许多项目依赖于容易被忽略的传递依赖。识别这些隐藏依赖有助于安全、许可与构建可重复性，需要通过分析二进制及其动态/静态依赖来绘制依赖图。</p>
